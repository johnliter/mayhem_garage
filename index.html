About Mayhem Garage

Established in 2024, Mayhem Garage is Kempner’s trusted auto repair shop. Our team, with a combined total of over 20 years of experience, is dedicated to delivering honest, high-quality service for every vehicle that enters our shop.

We specialize in gas, diesel, and small engine repairs, using only premium parts and transparent pricing.

From routine maintenance to complex diagnostics and lift kit installations, we treat every job with precision and care, ensuring your vehicle runs at its best.

We also sell rims with custom offsets and tires tailored to fit your lift kits, enhancing your vehicle’s style and performance.

As an authorized Rough Country Dealer, we also offer professional installation of Rough Country lift kits for all compatible vehicles.

Frequently Asked Questions

What vehicles do you service?

We service all makes and models of gas, diesel, and small engine vehicles and equipment, except German cars (e.g., BMW, Mercedes, Audi).

Do you offer warranties?

We offer 6-12 month warranties on parts and labor, depending on the manufacturer’s warranty.

How do I claim the hero discount?

Present a valid ID for military, veteran, first responder, or educator status at the time of service to receive 10% off.
